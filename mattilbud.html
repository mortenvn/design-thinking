Food discounts

Mattilbud is an app that keeps track of all the discounts at all the big food stores in Norway.

Download the app from Play Store for Android or App Store for Apple devices.

The first time you open the app, you need to enter your county. Click where it says “Oslo”, and change to “Sør-Trøndelag”.

The main page of the app looks something like this. Here, you can click on the image of the different food stores in Norway to see what is cheap this week. “Ny” means that there is a new discount paper available.

Each store has a different discount paper, but they all look something like this. They are all in Norwegian, but the images explain a lot.

Explaination of a few terms

  • 11.11-24.11 is the date for when the discount applies.
  • Pr. pk means per item
  • Pr. hg means per 100 grams
  • pr . kg means per 1 kilogram
